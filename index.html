Kakin Empire

The Kakin Empire (カキン帝てい国こく, Kakin Teikoku) is a country in the middle of the Azian Continent, and later also one of the countries of the V6.

The Fourteen Princes

Since gender has no bearing for succession, all the children are referred to as "Prince" — as in the "Nth Prince" in order of birth. All fourteen Princes are currently participating in a succession contest aboard the Black Whale during a voyage to the pretend New Continent where the sole survivor will be the official heir to the throne.
